Navisignal is the product ecosystem of JMPetrus Consulting — an evolving suite of practical tools designed to help leaders make sound decisions, strengthen organizational governance and accelerate impact in complex, risk-exposed environments.

Each tool encodes structured judgment into usable and defensible formats — diagnostics, frameworks, impact measurement, briefs, and decision supports.

Built for leaders who need decisions to hold up under scrutiny.

Built for teams that cannot afford reversible thinking.

What You'll Find Here

Tools designed for:

  • ESG and sustainability leaders navigating regulatory pressure
  • Governance teams facing ambiguity
  • Organizations managing reputation or operational exposure
  • Cross-functional teams making irreversible commitments

Formats may include:

  • Readiness diagnostics
  • Risk mapping tools
  • Decision briefs
  • Governance checklists
  • AI-enabled impact frameworks
