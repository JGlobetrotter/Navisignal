About

Navisignal is the product ecosystem of JMPetrus Consulting — an evolving suite of practical tools designed to help leaders make sound decisions, strengthen organizational governance, and accelerate impact in complex, risk-exposed environments.

Each tool encodes structured judgment into usable and defensible formats — diagnostics, frameworks, impact measurement systems, decision briefs, and governance supports.

When teams use Navisignal

Decision scenarios

  • Evaluating whether to bid on a complex donor-funded proposal
  • Assessing supplier readiness for CSRD and CSDDD compliance
  • Mapping human rights due diligence responsibilities across tiers
  • Structuring go/no-go decisions under time and resource pressure

Output types

  • Structured logframes with automated indicator tracking
