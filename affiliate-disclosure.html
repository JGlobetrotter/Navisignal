What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking links that allow a merchant to recognize that a customer was referred from our site. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the merchant pays us a small referral fee. This does not increase the price you pay.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Navisignal is built by JMPetrus Consulting as a resource for leaders navigating complex decisions. Affiliate partnerships help us:

Our Commitment to You

We only recommend products, services, and training programs that we genuinely believe provide value to our audience. Our recommendations are based on:

  • Relevance to governance, ESG, sustainability, and organizational decision-making

We will never recommend a product solely because of an affiliate commission. Our credibility depends on providing honest, useful guidance — and we take that seriously.

Where applicable, affiliate links on our site will be accompanied by a disclosure note such as:

  • "This is an affiliate link" or "affiliate link"
